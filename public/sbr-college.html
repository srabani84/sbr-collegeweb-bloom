PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

Welcome to Sashi Bhusan Rath Government Women's College, Berhampur

It is my privilege to welcome you to SBR Government Women's College—an institution dedicated to academic excellence, holistic development, and empowerment. Here, learning is a journey shaped by curiosity, compassion, and character.

We strive to provide a vibrant environment that nurtures leadership, innovation, and integrity. Our dedicated faculty, diverse programs, and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that every student receives the guidance and resources needed to excel in both academics and life.

Together, we foster a culture of inclusivity and lifelong learning, inspiring our students to become responsible citizens and confident professionals who contribute meaningfully to society.

DR. CHITTA RANJAN SATPATHY (PRINCIPAL)
